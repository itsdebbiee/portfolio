Competitive Analysis

One of the best and most effective ways for creating a website is to analyze what others with similar websites are doing. For my Cognitive Aspects of Digital Design class, we had to create a portfolio showcasing our work. The portfolio I have right now is the one I created for the class, albeit with a few more features than it had back then. I analyzed 10 different portfolio sites in regards to their Brand, Functionality, Interactivity, and Navigation. From this analysis, I was able to get a grasp of the current trends and decide what I ultimately wanted to include in my own portfolio. Analyzing competitor's portfolios and websites is a tremendously helpful skill that I still use to this day when creating a website.
