Rush Flyers

During my time as an active member of an Asian-interest sorority on campus, Sigma Omicron Pi, I contributed to our recruitment events as one of two Rush Chairs. As such, I coordinated several events that spanned a week and a half, as well as marketed through social media events and flyers. I created and help create a total of two separate flyers using Photoshop. These flyers can be seen here below.

Fall Rush

These were the rush flyers for the Fall Recruitment of 2012. For these particular images, I didn't do the actual Photoshop portion of it, but I designed the layout and worked closely with Eric Bui and instructed him on what I wanted for each particular design.

Winter Rush

These were the rush flyers for the 2014 Winter Recruitment period. I designed these flyers myself using Photoshop by using an image found on Google Images, which I then altered so that the Scrabble letters would show S, P, and I instead. Out of all the designs submitted, my flyer won and was printed into cardstock flyers to be distributed.
